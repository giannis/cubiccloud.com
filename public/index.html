Hello!

I am a passionate creator of software products and services that have a high social and economic impact,
based between Athens and Amsterdam.

Currently, I am the Chief Engineer of UX Information Technologies, an award-winning Mozambican startup developing high social and economic impact projects, where I guide and lead, mainly remotely, the small but very talented technical team.
Before that, I was a UX/Front-end Developer at Cloud9 IDE, one of the first cloud-based IDEs.
I have an MSc in Media Technology from Leiden University and a BSc in Electronic Engineering from TEI of Athens.

Projects

Mopa.co.mz

MOPA is a Participatory Monitoring platform for citizen engagement in waste management and other Municipal services. This platform connects citizens to the government using multiple communication channels like USSD, Apps and the internet.

Biscate.co.mz

A hybrid platform (USSD and Web) to connect blue-collar workers (e.g. painters, carpenters) to clients, and boost work opportunities in the informal sector.

Soma.co.mz

A data platform for training, creating and managing Savings Groups in rural areas. This system caters for the operational needs of a digital company that provides financial literacy and development opportunities to underserved populations.

Emprego.co.mz

The biggest job related website in Mozambique and the fastest growing website in the country.

Index.co.mz

An online search portal for Legislations, Jurisprudences and Doctrines for Mozambique.
